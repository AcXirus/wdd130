Ecuador

Ecuador is a country with diverse landscapes and cultures, located in the northwestern region of South America. Its territory includes the coast, the Andes, and the Amazon, and is famous for the Galapagos Islands. Guayaquil, its main port and second-largest city, is an economic and commercial engine of the country, known for its dynamism and growth. Quito, the capital, stands out for its historical heritage. Ecuador is a country rich in biodiversity and traditions.
